Overview —

RoBee M is Oversonic’s healthcare-focused humanoid robot. Built to work in clinical environments such as hospitals, rehabilitation centers, and elderly care facilities, it combines autonomous mobility, natural interaction, and data-driven intelligence.

It integrates seamlessly into existing healthcare workflows, without requiring technical training from staff or patients. With safety as a core principle, RoBee M is designed to operate even if accidentally pushed, hit, or obstructed—ensuring continuous reliability in sensitive contexts.

The name —

RoBee takes its name from the bee, a symbol of tireless work and collective intelligence. Like bees in a hive, RoBee is designed to operate with efficiency and precision as part of a network — collaborating with humans and with other robots.

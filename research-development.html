Research & Development

Concrete innovation, rooted in strategic collaborations with universities, research centers, and industrial partners. Our R&D explores new technologies for RoBee and their applications in medical, industrial, and aerospace domains.

Innovation through collaboration —

At Oversonic, Research & Development is the engine of our vision and the natural extension of our manifesto. We believe cognitive robotics should be a common good, designed to support people and empower their lives. This is why our R&D activity goes far beyond technical progress: it embodies Italian creativity, empathy, and a human-friendly approach to technology.

Our focus spans some of the most relevant domains of our time — including healthcare and neurorehabilitation, industrial transformation, and aerospace technologies — always with the same goal: creating robotic systems that cooperate with humans.

This approach is only possible through open collaboration. Our partnerships with universities, research institutes, and industrial players ensure that innovation is never isolated, but the result of shared expertise and collective vision. Working together, we build a research ecosystem that keeps technology aligned with human values, and transforms RoBee into a platform capable of evolving across different sectors, while remaining faithful to our guiding principle: Robotics for Humans.

Research fields —

Medical robotics & neurorehabilitation.

Villa Beretta Rehabilitation Institute
Joint project for using RoBee in neurological rehabilitation.
Tecnelab Il Cittadino MB La Provincia Unica
Joint laboratory with the Politecnico di Milano that aims to use RoBee to support patients in motor and cognitive recovery, reduce the workload of healthcare personnel, optimize rehabilitation therapies, and improve the psychological well-being of patients.
Fondazione Santa Lucia IRCCS
Trials with patients affected by neuro-motor disorders.
Santa Lucia Corriere della Sera TG5
The project is carried out by a multidisciplinary team of 24 professionals — 12 from Fondazione Santa Lucia and 12 Oversonic engineers — working together in a phased research program. RoBee is deployed directly in hospital wards and rehabilitation units.
Politecnico di Milano
Involvement in labs and applied research for clinical validation and development of rehabilitation protocols.

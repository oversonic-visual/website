The ecosystem —

At the core of RoBee lies Oversonic’s Cognitive Platform — a modular, AI-based software ecosystem designed to enable perception, reasoning, interaction, and safe operation in complex environments.
This platform brings together advanced vision, conversation, motion, and navigation systems in a unified architecture, ensuring humanoid robots can adapt to dynamic scenarios.

The Cognitive Platform is cloud-connected, GDPR-compliant, and built with enterprise-grade security (ISO 27001 certified). It integrates seamlessly with RoBee’s hardware and external IT infrastructures, enabling scalable deployment across industries.

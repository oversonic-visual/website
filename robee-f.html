Flow optimizer —

RoBee Series F is Oversonic’s humanoid cognitive robot designed specifically for intralogistics. Combining articulated bimanual manipulation, an autonomous omnidirectional base, and a rear module for transport and storage, RoBee F automates material flows in dynamic environments. It reduces cycle times, ensures traceability, and enhances workplace safety, acting as a collaborative resource in warehouses and production facilities.

The system architecture of RoBee F integrates humanoid dexterity with modular transport capabilities. Its vertical support extends operational reach across shelving and storage, while the rear module enables safe pallet and container handling. Combined with omnidirectional navigation, it provides a scalable solution for logistics in modern facilities.

Capabilities —

Key features.

➔ High-payload manipulation
7-axis articulated arms, 20 kg dual-arm payload, 120 cm reach.
➔ Vertical support system
Pneumatic lift with axial rotation for multi-level access.
➔ Autonomous logistics
Moves independently across hospital wards.
➔ Rear module
Standard europallets & containers; customizable for different loads.
➔ Autonomous navigation
Omnidirectional AMR/AGV base, SIL 3 obstacle detection.
➔ Vision & cognition
Cameras + AI for object recognition and corrective control.
➔ User interaction
Touchscreen, voicebot, visual signaling.
➔ Certified
ISO 9001, CE, EMC-EMI, IPX4, GDPR compliant.

The name —

RoBee takes its name from the bee, a symbol of tireless work and collective intelligence. Like bees in a hive, RoBee is designed to operate with efficiency and precision as part of a network — collaborating with humans and with other robots.
